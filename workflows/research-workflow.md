# Research Workflow

Use for current facts, tool choices, business planning, finance planning support, career research, entertainment planning, and technical decisions.

1. Define the decision the research supports.
2. Split the question into subquestions.
3. Use primary or high-quality sources first.
4. Check dates, versions, location, and applicability.
5. Separate facts, assumptions, and recommendations.
6. Summarize the practical choice and next step.

## Output

- Short answer.
- Key findings.
- Sources or evidence used.
- Caveats.
- Recommendation.
