# Business Planning Skill

## Purpose
Turn goals into a practical business plan or operating plan.

## Use When
Planning services, offers, financing, operations, marketing, hiring, or growth.

## Inputs
- Goal and timeframe.
- Customer and offer.
- Revenue/cost assumptions.
- Constraints and risks.

## Method
1. Define the business objective.
2. Clarify customer, pain, offer, and channel.
3. Model simple economics.
4. Identify risks and experiments.
5. Create a 30/60/90-day action plan.

## Output
- Strategy summary.
- Offer and customer.
- Financial assumptions.
- Action plan.
- Metrics.
