# Financial Planning Skill

## Purpose
Support personal budgeting, cash-flow planning, purchase decisions, savings goals, and scenario comparison.

## Use When
Planning budgets, comparing options, organizing expenses, preparing spreadsheets, estimating monthly impact, or thinking through personal finance decisions.

## Inputs
- Goal and timeframe.
- Assumptions the user chooses to share.
- Budget, income, expense, debt, or savings context.
- Preferred output format.

## Method
1. Clarify the decision or planning goal.
2. Separate known facts from assumptions.
3. Build simple scenarios.
4. Highlight tradeoffs, risks, and unknowns.
5. Recommend practical next steps and professional review when needed.

## Output
- Assumptions.
- Scenario table or plan.
- Risks and tradeoffs.
- Next actions.
- Professional-review note when appropriate.
