# Troubleshooting Skill

## Purpose
Turn vague symptoms into a reproducible diagnosis and safe fix.

## Use When
Something is broken, slow, flaky, or behaving differently than expected.

## Inputs
- Symptom and expected behavior.
- Scope of impact.
- Recent changes.
- Logs, errors, screenshots, commands, or timestamps.

## Method
1. Define the failure precisely.
2. Establish baseline and scope.
3. Check recent changes first.
4. Test one layer at a time.
5. Change one variable at a time.
6. Verify resolution and document prevention.

## Output
- Problem statement.
- Evidence.
- Most likely cause.
- Fix or next test.
- Prevention notes.
