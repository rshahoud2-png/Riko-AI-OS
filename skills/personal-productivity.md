# Personal Productivity Skill

## Purpose
Turn scattered tasks and goals into a realistic personal plan.

## Use When
Planning a day, organizing projects, breaking down goals, cleaning up tasks, designing routines, or creating a weekly review.

## Inputs
- Tasks or goals.
- Time available.
- Deadlines and constraints.
- Energy level or priority preferences.

## Method
1. Clarify the outcome.
2. Sort tasks by importance, urgency, and energy.
3. Break goals into next actions.
4. Create a realistic schedule or checklist.
5. Add a review point.

## Output
- Priorities.
- Next actions.
- Schedule or checklist.
- Blockers.
- Review cadence.
