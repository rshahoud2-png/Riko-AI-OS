# Resume Writing Skill

## Purpose
Create truthful, targeted, ATS-friendly career materials.

## Use When
Writing resumes, cover letters, LinkedIn summaries, or interview stories.

## Inputs
- Target role.
- Job description.
- Work history.
- Achievements and metrics.

## Method
1. Extract role requirements.
2. Match experience to evidence.
3. Rewrite bullets with action, scope, and result.
4. Tune keywords naturally.
5. Prepare interview proof points.

## Output
- Revised summary.
- Resume bullets or sections.
- Keyword notes.
- Interview stories.
