# Lifestyle Entertainment Projects Skill

## Purpose
Plan personal entertainment, lifestyle, travel, creative, and experience-based projects.

## Use When
Planning a trip, event idea, outing, gift, content idea, hobby project, or personal entertainment concept.

## Inputs
- Goal or occasion.
- Location, date, budget, and people involved.
- Preferences and constraints.
- Desired output: list, itinerary, budget, plan, or checklist.

## Method
1. Define the desired experience.
2. Gather constraints and preferences.
3. Research current options when needed.
4. Compare options by fit, cost, logistics, and risk.
5. Produce a plan with backup options.

## Output
- Recommended plan.
- Options compared.
- Budget/logistics notes.
- Checklist.
- Backup plan.
