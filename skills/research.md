# Research Skill

## Purpose
Produce decision-ready research from current, trustworthy sources.

## Use When
Facts may have changed, a tool must be selected, or a technical approach needs validation.

## Inputs
- Research question.
- Decision to support.
- Source constraints.
- Recency requirements.

## Method
1. Split into subquestions.
2. Search primary sources first.
3. Compare versions, dates, and authority.
4. Summarize evidence and uncertainty.
5. Recommend the next action.

## Output
- Short answer.
- Findings.
- Sources.
- Caveats.
- Recommendation.
